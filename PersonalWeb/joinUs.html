This game is a passion project that I have been working on for the past 2 years that started out as an idea one night after seeing the colors of the dice that we got. I know it's kind of a stretch a game from just colors but if you think about it wasn't the colors it was the creativity in me that was been fed from other games, stories, tales, movies, cartoons and the desire from my childhood to make a game that my family and friends can enjoy maybe even other people like all of you that are reading this right now, thank you for that, so since you read all of this and have been apart of the journey so far why not continue and join us. By joining the waiting list you are supporting me and motivating me to achieve my dream while sharing the dream of one day releasing this game. The waiting list is for me to know that you have been here from the start and for you to be notified of new updates I will be posting blog updates throughout the development so everyone can also give me suggestions and later on give me feedback when the game gets an alpha release, also everyone on the waiting list will get a special gift when the game releases.
