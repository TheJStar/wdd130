Dice Game

Thee dice game. A game where magic flows through your dice, your sitting in front of a table surrounded by your friends and all of you having the power to win the battle and embrace all glory. This game is a turn based strategy multiplayer where the last one standing wins. You all have 10 dice and 6 unique spells based on your character and you use the dice to cast spells and unleash chaos. It may seem like it's all random but through the ancient technique of dice freezing you can build your greatest attacks and combos with strategy and come out victories. This website is for introducing my game to everyone who visits it, keeping people informed on the progress on my game, the journey that it goes through, and later where you can download the game, give me feedback, get notified of events.
